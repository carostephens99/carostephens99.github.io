About

Hello, I'm Caroline

I make art because I can't not. Landscapes that catch my eye, cities I've wandered through, flowers on my table, faces I've imagined — it all ends up on paper or canvas eventually.

I'm self-taught and still learning, which means every piece is a little experiment. Some work out better than others. I'm sharing them all anyway.

Based in San Francisco. Painting in whatever time I can find.
